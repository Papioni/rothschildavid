Rothschildavid Nigeria Limited is a general contracting company. Our speciality varies depending on the needs of our clients. Clients have reached out to us on various projects namely building and construction, borehole drilling, website development, product supply, audio-visual studio development, studio sound proofing and so much more. We pride ourselves on the capacity to deliver any job given to us with speed and efficiency..

BACKGROUND

We chose the name 'ROTHSCHILDAVID' because it is associated with wealth, luxury and success. Rothschild being the name of one of the richest families on earth if not the richest is representative of some of the ideals we stand for which are honor, integrity and respect. Being an avid follower of the Rothschild development both as a family name and as business entities, we decided to name our company Rothschildavid. It must be said that it is in no way associated with the Rothschild family but be rest assured that when such association takes place we would make it public.
